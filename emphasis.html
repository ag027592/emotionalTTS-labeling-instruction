What to Listen For:

  • Words spoken louder than the others

Compare each word to others in the sentence. Which word stands out in terms of delivery?

Instructions:

  • Read the provided sentence.
  • Listen to the corresponding generated audio sample attentively.

  • Select that word as your response.
